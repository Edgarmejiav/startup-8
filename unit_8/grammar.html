A. Reduced Adverb Clauses

7. Rewrite: "After you finish the proposal, please send it to me."

  • a. After finish the proposal, please send it to me.
  • b. After finishing the proposal, please send it to me.
  • c. After finished the proposal, please send it to me.

8. Rewrite: "He continued working on his essay until he was satisfied."

  • a. He continued working on his essay until satisfied.
  • b. He continued working on his essay until satisfying.
  • c. He continued working on his essay until be satisfied.

9. Rewrite: "I often listen to podcasts as I commute to work."

  • a. I often listen to podcasts during commuting to work.
  • b. I often listen to podcasts as commuting to work.
  • c. I often listen to podcasts while commuting to work.

B. Participial Phrases

10. Rewrite: "Because I have a Mexican mother, I can speak Spanish fluently."

  • a. Because having a Mexican mother, I can speak Spanish fluently.
  • b. Having a Mexican mother, I can speak Spanish fluently.
  • c. To have a Mexican mother, I can speak Spanish fluently.

11. Rewrite: "Since I wasn't hungry, I didn't eat anything."

  • a. Not being hungry, I didn't eat anything.
  • b. Not to be hungry, I didn't eat anything.
  • c. No being hungry, I didn't eat anything.

12. Rewrite: "Since Isabelle is so talented, it's not surprising that she got promoted."

  • a. Giving that Isabelle is so talented, it's not surprising that she got promoted.
  • b. Being that Isabelle is so talented, it's not surprising that she got promoted.
  • c. Given that Isabelle is so talented, it's not surprising that she got promoted.

C. Nouns as Adjectives & Participial Adjectives

13. Thomas is a very ______ and is more than capable of handling the situation. (negotiator, trade, experience)

  • a. experience trade negotiator
  • b. experienced trade negotiator
  • c. experienced trading negotiator

14. This has been quite a ______ and I'm glad it's over. (project, time, consume)

  • b. time-consuming project
  • a. time-consumed project
  • c. consuming-time project

15. Keith gave an extremely long and dull presentation in front of several ______. (members, audience, disappointed)

  • a. disappointing audience members
  • b. disappointment audience members
  • c. disappointed audience members
