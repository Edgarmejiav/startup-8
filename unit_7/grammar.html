A. Negative Gerunds & Infinitives

7. You didn't return my call. That was rude.

  • a. Not returning my call was rude.
  • b. To not return my call was rude.
  • c. No returning my call was rude.

9. It's possible that I won't like my new job. I'm worried about that.

  • a. I'm worried about to not like my new job.
  • b. I'm worried about not liking my new job.
  • c. I'm worried not to like my new job.

B. Perfect Gerunds & Infinitives

10. It was a mistake to raise my voice in front of colleagues. (I regret...)

  • a. I regret to raise my voice.
  • b. I regret having raised my voice in front of colleagues.
  • c. I regret have raised my voice.

12. She was pleased because she was accepted into university.

  • a. She was pleased to have been accepted into university.
  • b. She was pleased having been accepted into university.
  • c. She was pleased to be accepted into university.

C. Reported Speech

13. They said, "We will see you again next week."

  • a. They told me they will see me again next week.
  • b. They told me that they would see me again the following week.
  • c. They told me that they would see me again next week.
