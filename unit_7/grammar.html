A. Negative Gerunds & Infinitives

7. You didn't return my call. That was rude.

  • a. Not returning my call was rude.
  • b. No returning my call was rude.
  • c. Not to return my call was rude.

8. I didn't see Anna again after she left the company. That's what I expected.

  • a. I expected to not see Anna again.
  • b. I expected not to see Anna again after she'd left the company.
  • c. I expected not seeing Anna again.

9. It's possible that I won't like my new job. I'm worried about that.

  • a. I'm worried about not liking my new job.
  • b. I'm worried about to not like my new job.
  • c. I'm worried not liking my new job.

B. Perfect Gerunds & Infinitives

10. It was a mistake to raise my voice in front of colleagues. (I regret...)

  • a. I regret having raised my voice in front of colleagues.
  • b. I regret to have raised my voice.
  • c. I regret having raise my voice.

11. I didn't mind speaking to George, because I know you didn't want to.

  • a. I'm happy to speak to George.
  • b. I'm happy to have spoken to George.
  • c. I'm happy having spoken to George.

12. She was pleased because she was accepted into university.

  • a. She was pleased to be accepted.
  • b. She was pleased to have been accepted into university.
  • c. She was pleased having been accepted.

C. Reported Speech

13. They said, "We will see you again next week."

  • a. They told me that they would see me again the following week.
  • b. They told me that they will see me again the following week.
  • c. They told me that they would see me again next week.

14. Bob asked, "What did the director say in his speech today?"

  • a. Bob asked me what did the director say.
  • b. Bob asked me what the director had said in his speech that day.
  • c. Bob asked me what the director said in his speech today.

15. Paula said, "I'm sorry I missed yesterday's meeting."

  • a. Paula said that she was sorry she had missed the previous day's meeting.
  • b. Paula said that she is sorry she missed yesterday's meeting.
  • c. Paula said that she was sorry she missed the previous day's meeting.
